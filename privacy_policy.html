  • 数据留存：录制文件保存在设备本地存储中，您可随时在应用内删除，或通过清除应用数据一并移除。
我们会在本地录制并保存您的音量数据以支持回放功能，但绝不将任何音频或音量数据上传至服务器或共享给第三方。

📋Overview

Thank you for using Decibel Monitor Pro (the "App"). This App is developed by an independent developer to provide users with professional ambient noise and sound level monitoring.

We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information. Please read this policy carefully before using the App.

Core commitment: This App does not collect or upload any personally identifiable information. Audio data is recorded and stored locally on your device for playback purposes only, and never transmitted to any server or third party.

🎤Microphone Permission

The App requires access to your device's microphone. This is the only permission necessary to deliver the core decibel-monitoring functionality.

  • Purpose: To capture ambient sound signals in real time and calculate and display decibel (dB) readings. The App also supports recording and saving sound level data locally for in-app playback.
  • Processing: Recorded sound level data is stored locally on your device only and is never uploaded to any server or third party.
  • Retention: Recorded files are stored in your device's local storage. You can delete them at any time within the App or by clearing the App's data.
We record and save sound level data locally to support the playback feature. This data is never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.

📊Data We Collect

The App does not collect any of the following:

  • Name, email address, or other personally identifiable information
  • Location data (GPS or network-based)

  • Photos, videos, or other media files

Data that may be stored locally on your device only:

  • User-defined app settings (e.g., display units, alert thresholds)
  • Historical decibel measurement logs and recorded sound level playback data saved by the user (stored locally only, never uploaded)

This local data is entirely under your control and can be deleted at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the App.
