We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information. Please read it carefully before using the App.

Core commitment: This App does not collect or upload any personally identifiable information. Wheel content you create is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

The App does not collect any of the following:

Name or personal identity
Email or account info
Location (GPS / network)
Unique device identifiers
Contacts / call logs
Photos / camera / microphone
